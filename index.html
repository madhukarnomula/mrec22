Dosa

How to make dosa

Ingredients

  • Parboiled rice
  • Urad dal
  • Fenugreek seeds (methi)
  • Water – as needed
  • Salt – to taste
  • Oil or ghee – for cooking dosa

Instructions

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and optional fenugreek seeds in water for 4–6 hours.
  2. Grind them to a smooth batter, mix, and let it ferment overnight.
  3. Pour and spread the batter on a hot pan, cook with a little oil until crispy.
  4. your dosa is ready to serve
